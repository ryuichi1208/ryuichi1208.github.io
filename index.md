# Ryuichi Watanabe

Active as a software engineer since 2016. Early in my career, I worked on file system development for Linux, then moved into infrastructure engineering and SRE, supporting the operation of web services. I am currently working as an SRE on a conversational voice AI SaaS at IVRy, Inc. I have particular strengths in incident response and EOL (end-of-life) handling.
